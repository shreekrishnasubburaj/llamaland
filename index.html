I want to return my purchase! What do I do?

If you are not 100% satisfied with your purchase from Lla-ma land you can return your llama(s) for a full refund within 365 days of purchase. Returns must be in the state you received them, and in the original packaging. Some llamas may ship with an attached security tag. Llamas returned without the original security tag attached or a damaged tag may not qualify for a refund.

I would like to return a gift that was sent to me. How do I do that?

Lla-ma land is very sorry that your gift did not work for you. Please call the Lla-ma land Customer Loyalty Team at 1-800-927-7671 for assistance returning the llama. They will need some information in order to pull up the account that the gift was purchased on. You will have to provide their phone number, first and last name, or email address. Any gift item being returned will be refunded to the original purchaser's method of payment. However, we would be happy to assist you with a gift return. When a gift return is processed, we will provide you with a return label to send the item back. Once we have received the item and processed the return, we will issue a gift certificate to your account. Please call the Lla-ma land Customer Loyalty Team at 1-800-927-7671 for assistance.

How do I know my llama size?

If you are unsure of your llama size, you can measure your llama size. Because the llama is three-dimensional, any two-dimensional measuring tool, such as a ruler or Brannock device® can only approximate your true llama size.
